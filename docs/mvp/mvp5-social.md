# MVP 5 — Social (Community + Recommendations)

> Can the community drive growth organically?

## Depends On
- MVP 4 (transactions create verified customers who can recommend)

## Goal
Power users become recommenders. Social proof drives new user acquisition without marketing spend.

## Features
1. **Recommendation Cards** — Shareable cards: "Kavitha recommends Lakshmi's Kitchen"
2. **Local Expert Badge** — Power recommenders earn badges, their picks rank higher
3. **Referral Program** — Refer customers → earn discounts
4. **Neighborhood Leaderboard** — Top recommenders this month
5. **Verified Purchase Reviews** — Reviews tagged with order proof

## Validates
- Do recommendations drive new signups?
- Do users trust peer recommendations over ratings?
- Does gamification (badges, leaderboard) increase engagement?

## Not Included
- No monetary referral rewards
- No merchant-to-merchant features
