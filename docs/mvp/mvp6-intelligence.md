# MVP 6 — Intelligence (Voice, Video, Festivals)

> Can the app serve non-tech-savvy users and seasonal demand?

## Depends On
- MVP 5 (needs critical mass of merchants and content)

## Goal
Unlock the next tier of users (non-literate, senior, festival shoppers) and the highest-intent moments (festivals, life events).

## Features
1. **Voice Search** — Speak freely → structured search (STT + LLM + TTS)
2. **Merchant Video Intro** — Guided 30-sec intro with auto-subtitles
3. **Festival Planner** — Checklist tied to upcoming festivals, pre-booking
4. **"I Need..." Button** — Post a need, relevant merchants get notified
5. **Merchant Insights** — "Most inquiries on Thursday evenings", revenue calendar
6. **Translation Bridge** — User speaks Tamil, merchant speaks Kannada → app translates

## Validates
- Does voice search increase conversion for non-literate users?
- Do video intros increase merchant selection rate?
- Does festival planner drive seasonal revenue spikes?

## Not Included
- Full accessibility mode
- Group orders from apartments
- Merchant-initiated micro-festivals
