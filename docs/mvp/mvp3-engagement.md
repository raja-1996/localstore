# MVP 3 — Engagement (Chat + Service Posts)

> Can merchants and users interact inside the app?

## Depends On
- MVP 2 (trust signals help users decide who to message)

## Goal
Users message merchants directly. Merchants post updates to keep followers engaged. All communication moves from WhatsApp into the app.

## Features
1. **Chat / Inquiry** — 1:1 text chat, chat list, unread indicators
2. **Service Posts** — Merchant posts with text + photo, optional service card attachment
3. **Push Notifications** — New message alerts, new posts from followed merchants

## Validates
- Do users prefer in-app chat over calling/WhatsApp?
- Do merchant posts drive repeat visits?
- Does push notification bring users back?

## Not Included
- No voice messages, no photo sharing in chat
- No polls, before/after posts
- No payments or booking
